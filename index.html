THE WITCHER

OVERVIEW
Geralt of Rivia, a mutated monster-hunter for hire also known as "Witcher", journeys toward his destiny and struggles to keep his humanity in a medieval fantasy world ruled by corrupt kings, queens and mages, where poverty, violence and introlerance are rampant and people often prove more wicked than beasts.
Featuring: Henry Cavill as Geralt of Rivia, Anya Chalotra as Yennefer, and Freya Allan as Ciri
Creator: Lauren Schmidt Hissrich
Genres: Fantasy TV Shows, TV Shows Based on Books, TV Dramas, US TV Shows, TV Action & Adventure
Maturity rating: 16+
